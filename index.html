Privacy Policy for CodeQuest

Effective Date: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

CodeQuest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address, full name, and profile information you provide during registration
  • Academic Data: Your progress in lessons, quiz scores, completed challenges, and learning statistics
  • Authentication Data: Information from Google Sign-In if you choose to use this authentication method
  • Usage Information: Subject enrollments, badge achievements, and interaction with educational content

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the CodeQuest educational service
  • Track your learning progress and provide personalized educational content
  • Enable teacher-student interactions for enrolled subjects
  • Display leaderboards and achievement badges
  • Send daily quiz notifications and learning reminders
  • Improve our services and develop new educational features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing user profiles, progress data, and educational content
  • Firebase Storage: For profile pictures and uploaded files

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Teachers: Teachers of subjects you are enrolled in can view your academic progress, quiz scores, and assignment submissions for educational purposes
  • On Leaderboards: Your username and scores may appear on class leaderboards (no email or sensitive personal information is shown)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Third-Party Services

CodeQuest uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, cloud storage, and analytics
  • Google Sign-In: For optional authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

7. Children's Privacy

CodeQuest is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the app settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications and certain data collection features

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using CodeQuest, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

11. Cookies and Tracking

CodeQuest may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze app usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to registered users
  • Posting the updated policy with a new effective date

Your continued use of CodeQuest after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable data protection laws
